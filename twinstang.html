Twinstang url

2008

Twinstang is a web application for pilots to digitally log and track their flight hours. It is built with Ruby on Rails and also works on the iPhone.

I built Twinstang while I was earning my private pilot license. I used it as a tool for tracking my own flight hours. Approximately 100 other beta users also used it. The response was good, but I never got it out of beta.

Technologies include:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • Mongo DB
  • Carrierwave
